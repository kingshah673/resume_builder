## Slide 1 - Myth #1
**❌ MYTH**: The ATS auto-rejects your resume if you don't have the right keywords.

**✅ TRUTH**: ATS systems are just databases. They don't reject anyone. If you didn't hear back, a human probably never even opened your application.

**The real problem? Invisibility — not rejection.**

---

## Slide 2 - Myth #2
**❌ MYTH**: You need an "ATS-compliant" score of 80%+ to get through.

**✅ TRUTH**: That percentage is invented by third-party tools like JobScan. No recruiter uses it. It doesn't come from an ATS. It's a metric designed to sell you a service.

**You don't need a higher score. You need a clearer message.**

---

## Slide 3 - Myth #3
**❌ MYTH**: The "white font trick" — hiding keywords in invisible text — will fool the ATS.

**✅ TRUTH**: ATS systems extract all text. When a recruiter shares your resume, that hidden text shows up in full. You go from "potential hire" to "red flag" instantly.

**If you have a skill, just show it with a real example.**

---

## Slide 4 - Myth #4
**❌ MYTH**: Modern ATS systems use AI to automatically screen and rank candidates.

**✅ TRUTH**: Most ATS platforms are 20–30 years old. They track applications, they don't judge them. Legally, companies cannot use AI to make fully automated hiring decisions.

**Write for a person, not a platform.**

---

## Slide 5 - Myth #5
**❌ MYTH**: Getting silence after applying means the system filtered you out.

**✅ TRUTH**: Hundreds of people apply daily. Your resume may simply never have been seen by human eyes. It's not rejection — it's a volume problem.

**Optimize for attention. Be scannable in 5 seconds.**
