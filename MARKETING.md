# Simple Marketing Strategy: Resume Builder (Honest Edition)

Based on the strategy: **Goals, Personas, Channels, and Tasks.**

## 1. Goals
- **Empower Job Seekers**: Provide a fast, free, and high-quality resume builder.
- **Build Radical Trust**: Debunk ATS myths and focus on human readability.
- **Honesty**: No "white font" hacks, no "fake scores". Just clarity.

## 2. Personas (Who are we helping?)
- **The "Myth-Buster"**: Job seeker tired of being told they need to "hack" the ATS. They want a clean, professional result.
- **The Career Strategist**: Focuses on business impact and visibility over keyword density.
- **The Busy Recruiter**: The ultimate "audience" who values scannable, clear resumes.

## 3. Channels (Where to reach them?)
- **LinkedIn**: Sharing insights about why "Invisibility is the problem, not rejection."
- **GitHub**: For transparency and open collaboration.
- **Educational Content**: Posting "Honest Resume Tips" derived from industry experts (like Farah).

## 4. Key Marketing Messages (ATS Truths)
- **Myth**: ATS auto-rejects you. **Truth**: No human has likely seen your resume because it lacked clarity.
- **Myth**: You need an "ATS-compliant" score. **Truth**: You need a Reader-compliant layout.
- **Myth**: White font works. **Truth**: It makes you look like a risk to recruiters.
